# Tag / Type / Keyword — Engine Audit

_Generated by the  workflow (8 Sonnet auditors + Opus synthesis), 2026-06-04. 116 findings, 86 gaps/partials across 8 categories._

## Summary

The engine auto-resolves a broad slice of card text via three layers: a regex effect parser (effects.ts parse/ParsedEffect), a trigger-pattern matcher (triggers.ts PATTERNS/TriggerEvent), and a state-aware cost reducer (autopay.ts effectiveCostOf), with hand-coded handlers in engine.ts fireTriggers for marquee champions/legends. The findings confirm the parser handles common single-clause effects well but systematically drops: (1) CONDITIONS on effects/enter-ready/keywords (the condition enum has only handAtMost/handAtLeast/unitsHereAtLeast/xpAtLeast/excessAtLeast, and `baseReady` strips all conditional guards), (2) TRIBE/TAG counting and tag-naming (no controlsTribe / all-tags / for-each-tag arithmetic anywhere), (3) several COST-REDUCTION shapes (third-person tag auras, "if you choose", per-tag/per-Equipment scaling), (4) whole missing TriggerEvents (recycle, attach, spend-buff, gear-ability-use, played-from-Hidden), (5) Equipment-as-a-keyword-source auras (type-scoped, "give" verb), (6) several missing destination/recursion targets (token "here" for recruits, opponent-spawn, Champion Zone, play-spell-from-trash, gear self on-play). Tag/type handling is the weakest area: card TYPE filters in "when you play a <type>" work, but creature-TAG synergy (Bird/Cat/Dog/Poro, Dragon, Mech, Equipment) is almost entirely unmodeled at the condition, cost, and keyword-grant levels. Highest leverage fixes are a generic enter-ready condition evaluator and a tribe/tag-count condition kind, which together unblock ~20 cards.

## Prioritized gaps (13)

### 1. [HIGH] Conditional enter-ready is never evaluated — baseReady regex matches "I enter ready" unconditionally, ignoring every printed guard

**Cards:** Direwing (sfd-094-221), Breakneck Mech (sfd-071-221), Vayne - Hunter (ogn-035-298), Towering Pairofant (unl-008-219), Dunebreaker (sfd-027-221), Xin Zhao - Vigilant (sfd-176-221), Shadow Watcher (unl-037-219), Shadow (unl-194-219)

**Fix:** In PLAY_UNIT (engine.ts ~line 3564), replace the bare `/\bi enters? ready\b/` test with a parser that detects a trailing condition clause ("if you control another <Tag>", "if you have N or fewer cards", "if you have N+ other units in your base", "if a unit died this turn", "if an opponent controls a battlefield", "if you play me to a battlefield"). Add a small `enterReadyConditionMet(s, p, card, toBattlefield)` helper reusing controlsTag (autopay), hand-size, base-unit-count, and a new turn-scoped `unitDiedThisTurn` flag on MatchState. Default entersReady to false when a guard is present and unmet. For Shadow specifically, the guard ("played to a battlefield") is unreachable without Ambush, so it must always enter base exhausted.

### 2. [HIGH] Tribe/tag counting & tag conditions are unmodeled — no condition kind for "control a <Tribe>", "all 4 tribe tags present", or "for each tribe tag", so gated effects fire unconditionally and dynamic scaling collapses to the literal number

**Cards:** Friendship (unl-046-219), Daisy! (unl-196-219), Poro Herder (ogn-061-298), Ivern - Friend to All (unl-177-219), Flurry of Feathers (unl-044-219), Unsung Hero (opp-167-221), Lonely Poro (sfd-036-221), Loyal Poro (unl-156-219)

**Fix:** Extend ParsedEffect.condition kinds with `controlsTribe` (value=tag string), `allTribeTags` (Bird AND Cat AND Dog AND Poro), `wasMighty`, `diedAlone`/`diedNotAlone`. Wire conditionMet (engine.ts ~line 361) to evaluate them against controlled units' tags and (for death gates) against bfIndex + a recorded mightOf-at-death snapshot. Add a `tribeTagCount` multiplier field so Friendship's "+1 Might per distinct tribe tag" and Daisy's per-tag cost cut read the live count (0-4) instead of the literal 1. Record died-with-others using the bfIndex already passed into fireTriggers.

### 3. [HIGH] effectiveCostOf is missing several cost-reduction shapes: third-person tag auras ("Your <Tag>s cost N less"), target-condition discounts ("if you choose a <Tag>"), and per-tag/per-Equipment scaling

**Cards:** Herald of Scales (ogn-140-298), Undying Loyalty (unl-168-219), Daisy! (unl-196-219), Stalking Wolf (unl-166-219)

**Fix:** In autopay.ts effectiveCostOf, add: (a) an owner-wide aura pass that scans controlledPermanents for "your <tag>s' energy cost(s) (are) reduced by N" / "your <Tag>s cost N less" and applies it when the card being costed carries <tag> (Herald of Scales → Dragon); (b) a "costs N less if you choose a <Tag list>" branch that reduces cost when the play's chosen target carries one of the tags (Undying Loyalty — needs the chosen target threaded into the cost call); (c) a "reduce my cost by N for each of the following tags ..." branch that multiplies N by the distinct-tribe-tag count among controlled units (Daisy). Reuse the controlsTag helper and the new tribe-count helper.

### 4. [MED] Additional play cost "kill/sacrifice a tagged unit" is unenforced, and the follow-on alternate entry-battlefield is unimplemented

**Cards:** Stalking Wolf (unl-166-219), Bard - Mercurial (sfd-079-221)

**Fix:** In PLAY_UNIT (engine.ts ~line 3524, alongside Accelerate/Deflect/Repeat), add an optional/required additional-cost recognizer for "As an additional cost to play me, kill a <Tag>" (Stalking Wolf, required) and "You may exhaust your legend as an additional cost" (Bard, optional). Track whether each was paid on the action; for Stalking Wolf enforce the kill (offer a sacrifice prompt over friendly tagged units) and allow entry at the dead unit's former battlefield; for Bard gate the on-play move-any-number effect on the legend-exhaust payment flag. Add a `move any number of units` parse form (currently moveUnit requires a/an/target/up-to-N).

### 5. [MED] No "play a SPELL from trash" path — playUnitFromTrash only matches units; spell-from-trash and Champion-Zone returns fall to manual

**Cards:** Kai'Sa - Evolutionary (ogn-112-298), Fizz - Trickster (sfd-140-221), Hallowed Tomb (ogn-281-298)

**Fix:** Add a ParsedEffect.playSpellFromTrash field mirroring playUnitFromTrash (maxEnergy cap, energyOnly, plus a `dynamicEnergyCap:'points'` option for Kai'Sa's "cost < your points"), and an applyParsed branch that resolves the spell's effect for free then recycles/trashes it. Separately, broaden returnFromTrash to allow a `to:'champion'` destination (Hallowed Tomb returns the Chosen Champion to p.champion when empty), keying off card.supertype==='champion'.

### 6. [MED] Recruit/token "here" placement is dropped for recruits — recruits field has no `here` flag so move/destination-triggered spawns land at base; multi-token spells/abilities also ignore battlefield-distribution choices

**Cards:** Noxian Drummer (ogn-222-298), Corina Veraza (sfd-179-221), Recruit the Vanguard (ogs-015-024), Vanguard Armory (sfd-168-221), Assembly Rig (sfd-019-221)

**Fix:** Give ParsedEffect.recruits a structured form `{count, here}` (or add a parallel `recruitsHere` flag) the way namedToken already carries `here`. In applyParsed's recruit branch, when `here` and a sourceIid resolve to a battlefield (bfIndexOfUnit), push tokens to that battlefield's units + recomputeControllers (fixes Drummer/Corina). For Recruit the Vanguard / Vanguard Armory, surface a placement pendingChoice to distribute tokens across base + controlled battlefields. Separately, relax Assembly Rig's recycle cost regex `/recycle (\d+) from your trash/` to also match "recycle a unit from your trash" (count=1) so canActivateUnit enforces a non-empty trash.

### 7. [MED] Equipment-as-a-keyword-source auras are unmodeled — unitGrantedKeyword only matches unit-TAG "your <tag>s have [kw]", not the card-TYPE "your Equipment" nor the verb "give", and gear cards can't receive granted keywords

**Cards:** Lucian - Purifier (sfd-183-221), Jax - Unmatched (sfd-054-221), Azir - Emperor of the Sands (sfd-197-221), Captain Farron (ogn-015-298)

**Fix:** Generalize unitGrantedKeyword (engine.ts ~line 2651) to (a) accept the verb "give" as well as "have", (b) recognize the card-TYPE scope "your Equipment" by checking whether the unit has any attached [Equip] gear rather than a tag match (Lucian → equipped units gain [Assault]), and (c) add a positional "other friendly units here have [Assault]" aura path inside auraMightHere (Captain Farron). For Azir's Sand Soldiers, consult unitGrantedKeyword during PLAY_UNIT's Weaponmaster check. For Jax - Unmatched, add a gear-keyword-grant lookup at PLAY_GEAR so legend-granted [Quick-Draw] confers Reaction/auto-attach.

### 8. [MED] Conditional keywords are read unconditionally — parseKeywords strips [Assault]/[Ganking] brackets regardless of the "If you've discarded..." guard, and dynamic-count Assault isn't computed

**Cards:** Raging Soul (ogn-019-298), Ancient Warmonger (sfd-131-221)

**Fix:** Add a discarded-this-turn flag to PlayerState (set in discard paths) and re-gate Raging Soul's [Assault]/[Ganking] in mightOf/unitHasGanking the same way the "while I'm buffed" Ganking gate already works (engine.ts ~line 2666). For Ancient Warmonger, special-case dynamic Assault in mightOf: when role==='attacker' and the unit's text declares Assault scaling "for each instance", substitute the live enemy-unit-count at its battlefield instead of the static k.assault=1.

### 9. [MED] Missing TriggerEvents — several event types don't exist in the TriggerEvent union, so their triggers never fire (recycle, attach, spend-buff, gear-ability-use, played-from-Hidden)

**Cards:** Sivir - Battle Mistress (sfd-203-221), Karma - Channeler (ogn-235-298), Fae Dragon (opp-101-221), Jax - Unrelenting (sfd-119-221), Prize of Progress (sfd-075-221), Teemo - Strategist (sfd-230-221)

**Fix:** Extend TriggerEvent (triggers.ts line 17) and add PATTERNS for: `recycleRune` (fire from RECYCLE_RUNE — Sivir), `recycleCard` (distinct, runes-aren't-cards — Karma, fire from VISION_DECIDE/peekToHand recycle sites), `attachEquip` (fire from PLAY_GEAR attach + the Weaponmaster/Jax attach paths — generalize fireAttachEquip which currently name-checks only Aphelios), `spendBuff` (fire when applyParsed/abilities remove a buff counter — Fae Dragon), `gearAbilityUse` (fire from ACTIVATE_UNIT when the source is a gear — Prize of Progress), and a `fromHidden` flag on the play event so REVEAL also fires defend-or-hidden triggers (Teemo - Strategist).

### 10. [MED] Self-scope on-play/reveal effects on GEAR never fire — PLAY_GEAR and the gear REVEAL branch call only firePlayTriggers (global, other permanents) and skip onPlayEffect/applyParsed for the gear itself

**Cards:** Forge of the Future (ogn-212-298), Shurelya's Requiem (sfd-192-221), Edge of Night (sfd-139-221), Gutter Palace (unl-088-219)

**Fix:** In the PLAY_GEAR handler (engine.ts ~line 3646) and the gear branch of REVEAL (~line 3834), call `applyParsed(s, p, onPlayEffect(card), ..., ci.iid)` for the gear so its own "when you play this" effect resolves (Forge → spawn Recruit, Shurelya → ready your units, Edge of Night → auto-attach on reveal). Also fix unitActivatedAbility's cost separator so a discard-before-exhaust cost ("Discard 1, :rb_exhaust::") parses for Gutter Palace's gear ability, and route Edge of Night's reveal through attach logic rather than dumping it unattached to base.

### 11. [MED] Opponent-directed and per-opponent effects are unmodeled — spawnNamedToken/spawnRecruits always create for the controller, and "on an opponent's turn" / "choose an opponent" gates aren't parsed

**Cards:** Walking Roost (unl-130-219), Viktor - Innovator (ogn-117-298), Scuttle Crab (unl-053-219), Noxus Saboteur (ogn-018-298)

**Fix:** Add an `opponentToken` effect form and let spawnNamedToken/spawnRecruits accept a target player (Walking Roost → opponent plays a Bird with [Deflect]). Fix clauseAfter so multi-sentence opponent effects aren't truncated at the first period (Walking Roost loses its second sentence; Scuttle Crab leaks its on-play "draw 1" into the death clause). Gate Viktor - Innovator's play:global trigger on `s.activePlayer !== player` (opponent's turn only). For Noxus Saboteur, add a REVEAL restriction scan: reject reveal when an enemy Noxus Saboteur shares the battlefield.

### 12. [LOW] Second activated ability and re-seat/already-attached Equipment handling are dropped — unitActivatedAbility cuts everything after the first sentence-period, and Weaponmaster only searches hand and never charges multi-component gear

**Cards:** Jax - Grandmaster At Arms (sfd-193-221), Azir - Ascendant (sfd-050-221), Yone - Blademaster (sfd-116-221), Armed Assailant (sfd-002-221), Sentinel Adept (sfd-008-221), Teemo - Swift Scout (ogn-263-298)

**Fix:** Let unitActivatedAbility return MULTIPLE abilities (split on "::" occurrences) so Jax's second exhaust-only re-seat ability is surfaced, and make the attach branch in ACTIVATE_UNIT able to target already-attached Equipment (search units' attached arrays, not just base). For Weaponmaster (PLAY_UNIT ~line 3630), charge the Equip cost minus one rainbow rune instead of free-attaching (multi-component gear still pays Energy) and allow re-seating already-attached gear. For Teemo - Swift Scout's bounce, extend bounceUnitToHand to also search p.champion (Champion Zone). Azir - Ascendant's position-swap remains a bespoke handler.

### 13. [LOW] Several bespoke single-card mechanics with no parser representation and no hand-coded handler (true one-offs)

**Cards:** Twisted Fate - Gambler (ogn-200-298), Heimerdinger - Inventor (ogn-111-298), Gearhead (sfd-068-221), Svellsongur (sfd-059-221), Strike Down (sfd-107-221), Rell - Magnetic (sfd-024-221), Royal Entourage (sfd-039-221), Ivern - Green Father (unl-195-219), Adaptatron (ogn-056-298), Blitzcrank - Impassive (ogn-067-298), LeBlanc - Fragmented (unl-172-219), Rek'Sai - Breacher (sfd-029-221), Ember Monk (ogn-167-298), Rift Herald (unl-179-219)

**Fix:** These need targeted handlers rather than a shared system: add hand-coded fireTriggers branches for Twisted Fate (reveal top rune, branch on domain), Heimerdinger (enumerate friendly exhaust abilities), Adaptatron/Blitzcrank (gate buff on optional gear-kill / gate move on played-to-battlefield, parse "return me"), Rell/Royal Entourage/Strike Down (free-Equipment-play, ready/exhaust-legend field, equipped-unit deals-its-Might + detach). Add host-aware multipliers to gearMight for Gearhead (double attached bonus) and a live text-copy mechanism for Svellsongur. Add a Brush battlefield-token + tag-aura for Ivern - Green Father. Add Rek'Sai - Breacher's source-origin Accelerate aura at all non-hand play sites. Fix Ember Monk by honoring the "from [Hidden]" qualifier in playTriggerMatches, and fix LeBlanc - Fragmented / Rift Herald by preventing on-play/on-move text from leaking into the stripped Deathknell string (parse the post-keyword clause, not the whole card).

### Dragon synergy engine audit

- ❌ **Herald of Scales** (ogn-140-298) — _Static cost-reduction aura: reduce the Energy cost of every Dragon card the controller plays by 2 (minimum 1). This is an owner-wide modifier that must be consulted by effectiveCostOf whenever a Dragon-tagged card is costed._ → effectiveCostOf in src/engine/autopay.ts has no code path for an aura-style 'your <tag> costs N less' pattern. The five cost-reduction patterns it recognises are: per-card-in-trash, per-cards-played-this-turn, tag-control self-discount ('costs N less if you control a <Tag>'), opponent-stunned conditional, and flat 'i cost N less'. None match Herald of Scales' third-person aura text ('Your Dragons' Energy costs are reduced by…'). Herald of Scales carries no trigger text and no on-play effect, so it is also invisible to parseTriggers and onPlayEffect. Its cost-reduction aura is completely unimplemented; Dragon cards always pay their printed Energy cost regardless of whether Herald of Scales is in play.
- ⚠️ **Direwing** (sfd-094-221) — _Conditional enter-ready: Direwing enters play ready only if the controller already controls another Dragon unit. The condition 'if you control another Dragon' must be checked against in-play units at the moment Direwing is placed._ → The PLAY_UNIT handler in engine.ts (around line 3564) sets entersReady via: baseReady = /\bi enters? ready\b/i.test(card.text) && !scoreReadyM. Direwing's text 'I enter ready if you control another Dragon.' matches this regex unconditionally, so Direwing always enters ready. The only special-cased conditional enter-ready patterns are Leona - Zealot (score-proximity check) and Monch (opponent-stunned check). There is no generic 'if you control another <Tag>' conditional enter-ready check, so the Dragon-control condition on Direwing is silently dropped.
- ⚠️ **Gentle Gemdragon** (unl-104-219) — _Two-part trigger: (1) when Gentle Gemdragon itself is played → ready up to 2 runes; (2) whenever the controller plays any OTHER Dragon while Gentle Gemdragon is already in play → also ready up to 2 runes. Both halves must fire._ → parseTriggers in src/engine/triggers.ts matches the self-scope pattern 'when(?:ever)?\s+you\s+play\s+(?:me|this)' against 'When you play me or another Dragon…'. This correctly registers a play:self trigger. The clause extracted after the match is 'or another Dragon, ready up to 2 runes', and parseEffectText finds readyRunes=2, so part (1) fires correctly on Gemdragon's own play. However, no global:play trigger is registered: the global pattern '/when(?:ever)?\s+you\s+play\s+(?:a|an|another)\b/' does not match the text (the 'another' appears after 'me or', not immediately after 'play'). The seen-set would also block a second play:global entry even if it did match. As a result, firePlayTriggers never fires Gemdragon's effect when the controller plays a subsequent Dragon; that half of the ability is a gap.
- ⚠️ **Fae Dragon** (opp-101-221) — _Two abilities: (1) on-play: buff up to 4 friendly units (standard buff aura, up to 4 targets); (2) reactive: whenever the controller spends a buff (removes a buff counter from any friendly unit), play a Gold gear token exhausted. The second is a 'spend-buff' triggered ability._ → Ability (1): parseTriggers matches play:self; clauseAfter yields 'buff up to four friendly units'; parse() sets buff=4 via the 'buff (?:up to NUM)? … units?' regex with group1='four'→4. applyParsed→applyBuff auto-applies up to 4 friendly buff targets. Fully handled. Ability (2): 'When you spend a buff, play a Gold gear token exhausted.' — there is no 'spendBuff' entry in TriggerEvent (src/engine/triggers.ts lists only play/conquer/hold/death/startOfTurn/attack/defend/move/winCombat/stun/enemyDeath/discard). No PATTERNS entry matches 'when you spend a buff'. The buff-spending code in applyParsed (e.spendBuff check at line ~34235 in engine.ts) is used only as a cost gate for Wildclaw Shaman-style 'spend a buff: effect on self', not as an event that fires triggers. The Gold token reaction is completely unimplemented and falls to manual resolution if surfaced at all.
