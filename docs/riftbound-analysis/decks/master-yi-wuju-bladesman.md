# Master Yi, Wuju Bladesman — Control · Solo-Hold Ambush

**Tier:** T1 · **Domains:** Calm + Body · **Win condition:** Lock down one battlefield with a solo defender boosted to +2 Might by the legend, then ambush-flash Rengar or Vilemaw to threaten a second point while protection spells keep the anchor alive until the opponent runs out of resources.

## Mulligan

**Keep:**
- Scuttle Crab (turn 1, 0-cost, draws a card and provides XP on death — never cut)
- Lonely Poro (turn 1 solo blocker that replaces itself on death if alone — ideal with legend)
- Pit Rookie (turn 2, buffs another unit, enables mid-combat math on the fly)
- Charm (turn-0 reaction to relocate a dangerous attacker away from your solo defender)
- Defy (counterspell protection for your defensive unit or gear)
- Discipline (cheap +2 / draw 1 reaction — keeps hand size and wins solo math)

**Toss:**
- Rengar — Trophy Hunter (expensive champion; good mid-game but a dead opener vs aggro)
- Vilemaw (8-cost late finisher — too slow turn 1)
- Sabotage (hand disruption is a mid/late game tool)
- Alpha Strike (win-more finisher; irrelevant when you need to survive turns 1–3)
- Tasty Faefolk (6-cost; fine to search but not needed in opening hand)
- Punch First (5-cost action; save for mid-game pump not early block math)

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish a defended battlefield

Deploy Scuttle Crab (`unl-053-219`) to your chosen anchor battlefield (prefer Vilemaw's Lair if available — units can't recall to base, locking attackers in). Scuttle Crab has 0 Might but can conquer and hold; it draws 1 card on entry. If you have a second rune, add Lonely Poro (`sfd-036-221`, 2 Might) to the same spot as the solo hold candidate.

**Intended outcome:** You control one battlefield, have drawn at least one extra card, and have a 2+ Might unit ready to solo-defend with the +2 legend bonus active next combat (giving effectively 4 Might on defence).

---

### Turn 2 — Fortify with the Chosen Champion

Play Master Yi — Tempered (`unl-113-219`, 4 Might, Hunt 2) to the anchor battlefield. He begins accumulating XP immediately on each hold/conquer. Hold back Charm and Defy as combat reactions. If the opponent deploys a unit adjacent, respond with Charm to move it away, preserving the solo-defend bonus.

**Intended outcome:** The anchor has a 4-Might threat that the legend's +2 pumps to 6 on solo defence. The Hunt 2 clock starts. You hold priority with two counters in hand.

---

### Turn 3 — Draw engine + second-battlefield pressure

Play Pit Rookie (`ogn-136-298`) to base or a second battlefield; its entry effect buffs a friendly unit (+1 Might permanent buff). Alternatively, play Tasty Faefolk (`ogn-075-298`) using its Accelerate cost — it enters ready and can attack or block the same turn. Use Discipline (`ogn-058-298`) as a reaction when your solo defender is challenged: +2 Might + draw 1 keeps you up in both board and cards.

**Intended outcome:** The anchor holds for a second consecutive turn (2 XP total for Tempered, approaching Level 6 at 6 XP). A second unit threatens a second point or acts as a sacrifice buffer so the legend's solo bonus stays on the anchor.

---

### Turn 4 — Midrange clock / ambush set-up

With 4–5 energy available: hold up 2 energy for Defy and deploy Scuttle Crab (if not already played) to a new battlefield, or replay Lonely Poro to a contested spot. If Master Yi — Tempered has reached Level 6 (6 XP), he gains Deflect + Ganking — opponents must pay an extra rune to target him, and he can chase across battlefields. At this point use Grim Resolve (`unl-095-219`) on him: +3 Might this turn, and if he wins combat you gain 2 more XP, jumping toward the Wuju Master upgrade threshold.

**Intended outcome:** The legend ability (solo +2) and Tempered's Deflect together make removal extremely taxing for the opponent. Two battlefields now contested. You have 4–6 XP total.

---

### Turn 5 — Ambush finisher / hand disruption

Flash Rengar — Trophy Hunter (`unl-120-219`, 6 Might, Ambush) as a Reaction to a battlefield where you have even one unit — including an enemy-held battlefield because his text allows it. The opponent must find an answer at instant speed or concede a conquest. Simultaneously, play Sabotage (`ogn-156-298`) to strip the opponent's best protection spell from hand.

**Intended outcome:** Rengar takes a second or third battlefield by Ambush while the anchor holds. You are 2–0 or 2–1 in points; opponent is playing from behind on resources.

---

### Turn 5+ — Close with Vilemaw / Alpha Strike

Deploy Vilemaw (`unl-060-219`, 8 Might, Ambush) as a late Reaction anchor on a battlefield where enemy units have less Might — his ability silences their combat damage entirely. When he holds, draw 1. At this stage play Alpha Strike (`unl-192-219`): choose Master Yi — Tempered (or Vilemaw), deal damage equal to Might split across all enemy units at battlefields, gain 1 XP per kill.

**Intended outcome:** Table locked; opponent cannot contest multiple battlefields with their Might totals blanked. Alpha Strike chains into XP that may trigger Wuju Master (`unl-191-219`) upgrade (Level 11: units enter ready, closing all lanes simultaneously).

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF a friendly unit is the **only** unit you control at a battlefield AND it **defends**, THEN Master Yi — Wuju Bladesman grants it +2 Might for that combat.
- IF Master Yi — Tempered **conquers or holds** a battlefield, THEN you gain 2 XP (Hunt 2 ability).
- IF Master Yi — Tempered has reached Level 6 (6+ XP), THEN opponents must spend one rune to choose him with a spell or ability (Deflect), AND he may move from battlefield to battlefield (Ganking).
- IF you play En Garde (`ogn-046-298`) on a unit that is the **only** unit you control at that battlefield, THEN it gains +2 Might total (+1 base +1 solo bonus) this turn instead of just +1.
- IF Lonely Poro (`sfd-036-221`) **dies alone** (no other friendly units at its battlefield), THEN you draw 1 card from its Deathknell.
- IF Scuttle Crab (`unl-053-219`) **dies**, THEN the chosen opponent reveals their hand, you see all their facedown cards this turn, and you gain 1 XP.
- IF Zhonya's Hourglass (`ogn-077-298`) is equipped (Hidden) AND a friendly unit would die, THEN kill the Hourglass instead, heal that unit, exhaust it, and recall it to base.
- IF Rengar — Trophy Hunter (`unl-120-219`) is played as an Ambush Reaction AND you have at least one unit at the target battlefield, THEN Rengar enters that battlefield even if only enemies are there (his special clause overrides normal Ambush restrictions).
- IF Vilemaw (`unl-060-219`) is at a battlefield AND enemy units there have **less Might than Vilemaw**, THEN those enemy units deal 0 combat damage.
- IF Vilemaw holds a battlefield, THEN you draw 1 card.
- IF you play Tasty Faefolk (`ogn-075-298`) using its Accelerate cost (1 energy + 1 Calm rune additional), THEN it enters ready and can attack or defend the same turn.
- IF Pit Rookie (`ogn-136-298`) is played AND the targeted friendly unit has no buff, THEN it gains a permanent +1 Might buff.
- IF Grim Resolve (`unl-095-219`) is cast on a unit AND that unit **wins a combat** this turn, THEN you gain 2 XP.
- IF Alpha Strike (`unl-192-219`) kills one or more units, THEN you gain 1 XP per kill.
- IF Akshan — Mischievous (`sfd-109-221`) is played paying the additional Body+Body rune cost AND the opponent has a gear, THEN move that gear to your base under your control; if it is equipment, attach it to Akshan.
- IF Star Spring (`unl-215-219`) is your battlefield AND a player plays their first non-token unit there in a turn, THEN that player may move another unit they control at Star Spring back to base (tempo safety valve protecting your solo-hold or clearing a blocker).
- IF The Arena's Greatest (`ogn-290-298`) is your battlefield AND a new Beginning Phase starts, THEN the active player gains 1 point automatically.
- IF Vilemaw's Lair (`ogn-295-298`) is your battlefield, THEN units cannot move from Vilemaw's Lair to their base (traps aggressive units deployed there; prevents opponent from safely recalling their attacker).
- IF Not So Fast (`sfd-045-221`) is held as a Reaction AND an enemy spell or ability **chooses** a friendly unit or gear, THEN counter that spell or ability entirely.
- IF Defy (`ogn-045-298`) is held as a Reaction AND an enemy spell costs ≤4 energy and ≤1 rune, THEN counter it.
- IF Sabotage (`ogn-156-298`) resolves, THEN the chosen opponent reveals their hand and you recycle one non-unit card from it, removing their best protection spell.
