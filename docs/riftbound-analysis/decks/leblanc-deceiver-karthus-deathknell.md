# LeBlanc - Deceiver — Karthus Deathknell

**Tier:** S / Top Tier · **Domains:** Mind + Order · **Win condition:** Chain exponential Deathknell triggers through Karthus doubling and Reflection tokens to draw the entire deck, channel runes, and remove blockers — then convert battlefield holds into a scoring avalanche with temporary Karthus copies.

## Mulligan

**Keep:**
- Watchful Sentry (`ogn-096-298`) — cheapest Deathknell engine; card draw on death is the deck's lifeblood
- Karthus - Eternal (`ogn-236-298`) — doubles every Deathknell trigger; finding him early changes the math of every subsequent turn
- Soaring Scout (`ogn-216-298`) — 2-energy Deathknell that channels a rune exhausted; smooths ramp to Karthus/Glasc Mixologist
- Seal of Unity (`ogn-245-298`) — 0-cost, reaction-speed Order rune channeling; lets you hit color requirements without tapping into your main rune pool
- Mirror Image (`unl-200-219`) — can copy Karthus or a Deathknell unit for a free trigger chain on your next Beginning Phase

**Toss:**
- Sacrifice (`unl-173-219`) — needs a Mighty (5+ Might) unit to exist; dead card in opening hand with no board
- Glasc Mixologist (`sfd-165-221`) — too expensive (5 energy) to be reliable Turn 1–2; better to dig into it naturally
- Black Rose Dignitary (`unl-152-219`) — competes with Soaring Scout for 3-drop slot but offers less immediate value without an active LeBlanc
- Ruined Rex (`unl-067-219`) — top of curve (6 energy); don't keep it unless you have a clear ramp path

## Turn-by-turn Game Plan

### Turn 1 — Establish a Deathknell unit

Play Soaring Scout (`ogn-216-298`) or Watchful Sentry (`ogn-096-298`) to any battlefield, ideally one you intend to contest. These 1–2-Might bodies are not expected to win combat; they exist to die profitably. Deploy at the battlefield that aligns with your Windswept Hillock target (so they can Gank to a more contested zone later if the board opens up). **Intended outcome:** 1 Deathknell unit on board, color requirements met.

### Turn 2 — Channel runes, prepare Karthus line

If you have Soaring Scout in play and it survives, move toward attacking. Otherwise play a second 2-drop (another Scout or Sentry) or hide a Hidden Blade (`ogn-213-298`) for :rb_rune_rainbow: to react with later. Play Seal of Unity (`ogn-245-298`) for 0 energy to bank an Order rune. **Intended outcome:** Rune base growing; removal hidden; board pressure low but present.

### Turn 3 — Land Karthus - Eternal or LeBlanc - Fragmented

This is the pivotal turn. Play Karthus - Eternal (`ogn-236-298`) at your base (3 energy, 1 Order rune). With Karthus in play, every subsequent Deathknell trigger fires twice. If Karthus is not in hand, play LeBlanc - Fragmented (`unl-172-219`, 3 energy, 1 Order): a 3/4-Assault unit whose Deathknell draws 2 (or 4 with Karthus) if it dies during your Beginning Phase. After winning or holding a battlefield, exhaust LeBlanc - Deceiver legend ability — discard 1 card (ideally something you want in the trash like a redundant Sacrifice) to create a ready Reflection copy of Karthus or LeBlanc - Fragmented at that battlefield. **Intended outcome:** Karthus in play OR Reflection copy of a Deathknell value unit positioned at a live battlefield.

### Turn 4 — Trigger Deathknell chain, draw mass cards

Reflection tokens created by LeBlanc's ability are Temporary — they die at the start of your Beginning Phase (before scoring). At Turn 4's Beginning Phase, if a Reflection of Watchful Sentry dies: draw 1 (×2 with Karthus = draw 2). If Reflection of LeBlanc - Fragmented dies: draw 4 (2 base ×2). If multiple Reflections die in the same Beginning Phase, all triggers resolve. Play Dusk Rose Lab (`unl-209-219`) as your battlefield if contested; its text lets you proactively kill a unit at the start of your Beginning Phase to draw 1 — useful when you want to fire a Deathknell before the natural Temporary death. Activate Mirror Image (`unl-200-219`, 3 energy, 2 Mind) to copy Karthus himself, doubling the multiplier: now every Deathknell fires three or more times. **Intended outcome:** 4–8 cards drawn in Beginning Phase; rune channels firing; board state reset to your favor.

### Turn 5+ — Close with Ruined Rex + Glasc Mixologist recursion

With a full hand, play Ruined Rex (`unl-067-219`, 6 energy, 1 Mind). Its Deathknell deals 4 damage to an enemy unit; with Karthus, that becomes 8 damage (or 4 damage split across 2 units). Use Cull the Weak (`ogn-209-298`, 2 energy, 1 Order) to kill Ruined Rex on demand while also killing an opposing unit — Deathknell fires, deal 4/8 damage. Glasc Mixologist (`sfd-165-221`, 5 energy, 1 Order) Deathknell lets you replay any unit from trash costing 3 energy or less for free — replay Karthus from trash for another doubling layer, or replay LeBlanc - Fragmented for more draws. Shard of Undoing (`unl-174-219`) on board: the first time a friendly unit dies during your Beginning Phase, each opponent must kill one of their units — chaining this with Reflection deaths forces board wipes you didn't need to pay for. Win by holding all three battlefields while opponents cannot field meaningful blockers after repeated removal triggers. **Intended outcome:** Opponent's board cleared; you hold ≥2 battlefields going into final scoring turns.

## Key Conditional Interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF a Reflection token created by LeBlanc - Deceiver's legend ability dies at the start of your Beginning Phase THEN its Deathknell effect triggers (because the token is a copy of the unit, it inherits that unit's Deathknell text).

- IF Karthus - Eternal (`ogn-236-298`) is in your base THEN every Deathknell effect triggers an additional time (e.g. Watchful Sentry draws 2, LeBlanc - Fragmented draws 4, Ruined Rex deals 8 damage or 4 to two units).

- IF you have two Karthus copies in play (original + Reflection via Mirror Image or LeBlanc ability) THEN each Deathknell triggers three times instead of two.

- IF LeBlanc - Fragmented dies during your Beginning Phase THEN its Deathknell draws 2 (base) or 4 (with Karthus), rather than drawing 1.

- IF you control Shard of Undoing (`unl-174-219`) AND a friendly unit dies during your Beginning Phase THEN each opponent must kill one of their units (triggers once per turn on the first death).

- IF you play Cull the Weak (`ogn-209-298`) THEN both you and each opponent kill one of their units — you choose to kill a Deathknell unit, triggering its effect.

- IF Deathgrip (`sfd-163-221`) is played and a friendly unit is killed THEN that unit's Might is added to another friendly unit's Might this turn AND you draw 1; use this to pump a small unit into Mighty range for Sacrifice eligibility.

- IF a friendly unit has 5+ Might (is Mighty) THEN Sacrifice (`unl-173-219`) can be played as a Reaction to kill it, draw 2, and channel 1 rune exhausted — you choose the Deathknell unit to kill.

- IF Mirror Image (`unl-200-219`) targets Karthus AND the Reflection dies next Beginning Phase THEN Karthus's passive is in play until that moment, effectively giving you a full extra turn of triple Deathknell triggers before the Reflection vanishes.

- IF Glasc Mixologist dies THEN you may play any unit from your trash with cost ≤3 energy and ≤1 rune of any color for free — ideal to replay Karthus (cost 3) or Watchful Sentry (cost 2) from the trash.

- IF Dusk Rose Lab is your controlled battlefield THEN at the start of your Beginning Phase you may kill a friendly unit there to draw 1 (before scoring) — use to proactively trigger a Deathknell unit rather than waiting for combat.

- IF Windswept Hillock is in play THEN all units there have Ganking, allowing them to move between battlefields — ready Reflection tokens placed at base can attack a battlefield and then gank to another, scoring 2 points in a single turn.

- IF Hidden Blade (`ogn-213-298`) is hidden for :rb_rune_rainbow: THEN it can be played for 0 energy as a Reaction to kill any unit at a battlefield (its controller draws 2) — use offensively to remove a blocker mid-combat chain or defensively to kill your own Deathknell unit and draw 2.

- IF Thousand-Tailed Watcher (`ogn-116-298`) is played with Accelerate (pay 1 energy + 1 Mind rune extra) THEN it enters ready and its "enter" ability triggers immediately — giving all enemy units -3 Might this turn.

- IF Tactical Retreat (`unl-175-219`) is played on a friendly unit AND that unit would die this turn THEN it is instead healed, exhausted, and recalled to base — use to save Karthus from removal or a Deathknell unit from being killed by Hidden Blade at an inopportune time.

- IF LeBlanc - Deceiver conquers or holds a battlefield AND you discard 1 AND exhaust LeBlanc THEN a ready Reflection token is placed at that battlefield as a copy of any other unit there — choose the highest-value Deathknell unit present.
