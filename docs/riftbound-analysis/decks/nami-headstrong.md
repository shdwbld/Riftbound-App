# Nami — Headstrong (Rogue · Calm/Order Stun-Chain Midrange)

**Tier:** T2 · **Domains:** Calm + Order · **Win condition:** Stack stun triggers to chain Leona's passive buff onto your board every turn, stalling the fastest enemy units while snowballing a buffed midrange army that holds and conquers battlefields faster than opponents can answer.

## Mulligan

**Keep:** Solari Shieldbearer (ogn-051-298) — guarantees a stun on turn 3 and immediately activates Leona's passive; Clockwork Keeper (ogn-044-298) — cheap 2-cost unit for early battlefield presence and rune channeling; Rune Prison (ogn-050-298) or Back Off (unl-042-219) — reactive stun held as hidden threat; Scuttle Crab (unl-053-219) — cantrip that draws a card and leaves a 0/0 that can still hold.

**Toss:** Eclipse Herald (ogn-059-298) without a stun piece in hand to activate it — at 7 energy it sits unplayable early; Solari Chief (ogn-225-298) before turn 5 when the conditional kill line is unavailable; Monch (unl-035-219) without a prior stun on the board — its cost-reduction requires an opponent's stunned unit; Zenith Blade (ogn-262-298) in the opening hand when you have no unit to move yet.

**Why:** The deck's engine starts on the first stun trigger — Leona's legend passive buffs a friendly unit every time you stun, so the earlier you land stuns the faster your board outscales opponents. You need at least one cheap unit (Clockwork Keeper, Scuttle Crab) to hold a battlefield and one stun source (Shieldbearer, Rune Prison, Back Off hidden) in the opening hand. The high-cost threats (Eclipse Herald, Monch, Solari Chief) are curve-toppers, not openers.

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Place a unit, hide a reactive stun

Play Clockwork Keeper (ogn-044-298) or Scuttle Crab (unl-053-219) to a battlefield. If you hold Back Off (unl-042-219), pay :rb_rune_rainbow: to hide it face-down at the same battlefield. If you have Zhonya's Hourglass (ogn-077-298), place it unreachable.

Scuttle Crab on turn 1 draws a card immediately on play — this is the best turn-1 play if the draw helps you hit a stun piece.

Intended outcome: A unit is on at least one battlefield. Back Off is loaded face-down as a 0-energy reaction for turn 2 showdowns. Leona legend cannot fire yet but the board is established.

### Turn 2 — Hold, trigger early stun if possible

If Solari Shieldbearer (ogn-051-298) is in hand, play it now (3 energy): its "when you play me, stun a unit" triggers immediately, firing Leona - Radiant Dawn (ogn-261-298) to buff a friendly unit with +1 :rb_might:.

If no Shieldbearer: hold with the Turn-1 unit and channel a Calm Rune. Consider playing Rune Prison (ogn-050-298) at 2 energy to stun the most threatening enemy unit before it attacks. That stun also triggers Leona's passive.

Intended outcome: Leona fires at least once (+1 :rb_might: on Clockwork Keeper or Scuttle Crab). The first stun is on board; opponents must plan around it. Your unit is already buffed heading into Turn 3 combat.

### Turn 3 — Stun chain begins; play Nami or Leona - Determined

**Line A — Nami line:** Play Nami - Headstrong (unl-052-219) for 3 energy, paying the additional :rb_rune_calm: cost to stun an enemy unit on entry. Leona fires again (+1 :rb_might: to a friendly). Nami holds next turn, readying and buffing the next unit you play that turn.

**Line B — Leona Determined line:** Play Leona - Determined (ogn-238-298) at 4 energy. On her first attack, she stuns an enemy unit here — triggering Leona legend passive (+1 :rb_might: to a friendly), and if Vex - Mocking (unl-055-219) is on board, Vex can move to that battlefield.

Use Defy (ogn-045-298) at 1 energy as a Reaction if an opponent tries to counter your stun piece.

Intended outcome: Two or more stun triggers have fired this game; board has 1-2 units each holding a +1 :rb_might: buff. Leona's passive has created a growing stat advantage.

### Turn 4 — Double-stun turn; deploy midrange threats

If Nami held on Turn 3: play any unit this turn and it enters ready AND gets a buff — ideal to play Solari Chief (ogn-225-298). Chief's entry effect checks whether its chosen enemy unit is stunned: if yes, kill it; if no, stun it. With a stun already on board (from Nami's entry), this becomes "kill the already-stunned unit" — removing a blocker for free and triggering Leona again.

Alternatively, play Leona - Zealot (ogn-079-298) at 6 energy: stunned enemy units at her battlefield lose 8 :rb_might: (minimum 1), making them trivially killable in combat.

Zenith Blade (ogn-262-298) at 3 energy as an Action: stun an enemy unit and move a friendly unit to that battlefield — repositioning Vex - Mocking (unl-055-219) costs nothing extra because Vex's own ability moves her when you stun.

Intended outcome: Two stun triggers this turn = two Leona passive buffs. The board has 2-3 units each with a +1 :rb_might: buff. An enemy blocker may be dead outright via Solari Chief. Points begin flowing.

### Turn 5+ — Eclipse Herald burst; close out with Monch

Play Eclipse Herald (ogn-059-298) at 7 energy: every time you stun an enemy unit, Eclipse Herald readies and gains +1 :rb_might: this turn — it can attack multiple times in the same turn if you chain stuns via spells (Rune Prison, Zenith Blade, Back Off). Each stun also fires Leona to buff more units.

Monch (unl-035-219) becomes available as soon as an opponent controls a stunned unit — pay 4 energy instead of 6 and it enters ready. An 6-might unit entering ready on Turn 5 becomes an immediate attacker.

Vi - Peacekeeper (unl-176-219) enters via Ambush (play her as a Reaction when opponents enter a battlefield you control) and stuns an enemy unit on her first attack — adding yet another stun trigger.

Solari Shrine (ogn-072-298) gear payoff: every time you kill a stunned enemy unit, exhaust Shrine to draw a card — Solari Chief's conditional kill and combat kills on stunned units fuel card draw.

Intended outcome: Board is 3-4 units each with a +2 to +4 :rb_might: buff from repeated Leona triggers. Eclipse Herald is attacking multiple times per turn. Opponents cannot stabilize against a fully buffed 5-7 :rb_might: Leona - Zealot that also drains 8 :rb_might: from any stunned unit she faces.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you stun one or more enemy units on your turn THEN exhaust Leona - Radiant Dawn (ogn-261-298) to give a friendly unit +1 :rb_might: (once per "stun one or more" event, not once per individual unit stunned).
- IF you play Nami - Headstrong (unl-052-219) AND pay the additional :rb_rune_calm: cost THEN stun an enemy unit on entry (triggers Leona passive immediately).
- IF Nami - Headstrong (unl-052-219) holds at a battlefield THEN the next time you play a unit that same turn, that unit enters ready AND receives a +1 :rb_might: buff.
- IF Leona - Determined (ogn-238-298) attacks THEN stun an enemy unit at her battlefield (triggers Leona passive; Vex - Mocking can reposition to that battlefield).
- IF Leona - Zealot (ogn-079-298) is at a battlefield AND an enemy unit there is stunned THEN that stunned unit has -8 :rb_might: (minimum 1), making it nearly always killable in combat.
- IF Solari Chief (ogn-225-298) is played AND the chosen enemy unit is already stunned THEN kill that unit instead of stunning it (Solari Shrine draw trigger fires if it dies while stunned).
- IF Solari Chief (ogn-225-298) is played AND the chosen enemy unit is NOT stunned THEN stun it (triggers Leona passive; sets up a kill next turn).
- IF Solari Shieldbearer (ogn-051-298) is played THEN stun any unit (triggers Leona passive on entry).
- IF Eclipse Herald (ogn-059-298) is at a battlefield AND you stun an enemy unit (by any means) THEN Eclipse Herald readies AND gains +1 :rb_might: this turn (can chain across multiple stuns in the same turn for multiple attacks).
- IF Vex - Mocking (unl-055-219) is on the board AND you stun an enemy unit at any battlefield THEN you may move Vex - Mocking to that battlefield (free repositioning every time a stun resolves).
- IF Vi - Peacekeeper (unl-176-219) is played via Ambush (as a Reaction to a battlefield where you have units) THEN she enters as a defender; when she attacks, she stuns an enemy unit here (triggers Leona passive and Vex movement).
- IF Back Off (unl-042-219) is hidden face-down AND played as a Reaction THEN it costs 0 energy and stuns a unit; the draw-1 clause does NOT trigger from face-down (only triggers when played from hand).
- IF Back Off (unl-042-219) is played directly from hand THEN it stuns a unit AND draws 1 card.
- IF Monch (unl-035-219) is played AND an opponent controls at least one stunned unit THEN Monch costs :rb_energy_2: less (from 6 to 4) AND enters ready (can immediately attack).
- IF Rune Prison (ogn-050-298) is played as an Action THEN stun any unit (triggers Leona passive; usable on your turn or in showdowns).
- IF Zenith Blade (ogn-262-298) is played THEN stun an enemy unit at a battlefield AND optionally move a friendly unit to that enemy's battlefield (triggers Leona passive AND Vex repositioning, plus allows a surprise hold takeover).
- IF Thwonk! (sfd-040-221) is played AND you pay the extra :rb_energy_2: Repeat cost THEN the stun effect resolves twice, potentially stunning two attackers in the same combat or locking down a key threat through the :rb_energy_2: Repeat.
- IF Skyward Strike (unl-038-219) is played AND you have 6+ XP THEN move an enemy unit AND stun a separate enemy unit (both effects resolve, generating two repositioning/stun opportunities and triggering Leona passive).
- IF you hold at Amateur Recital (unl-207-219) THEN you may move a unit at any battlefield to its base (repositioning a blocker or resetting a friendly unit without paying additional energy).
- IF you hold at Gardens of Becoming (unl-213-219) THEN you may play a Plant token or gain a resource advantage specific to that battlefield's effect (supports green/calm ramp into Eclipse Herald or Monch curves).
- IF Solari Shrine (ogn-072-298) is in play AND you kill a stunned enemy unit THEN exhaust Shrine to draw 1 card (rewards the Solari Chief + stun-kill line with card advantage).
- IF Zhonya's Hourglass (ogn-077-298) is equipped AND the equipped unit would die THEN the unit is instead set to 1 :rb_might: and prevented from dealing or receiving more combat damage that turn (protects a buffed key threat).
- IF Not So Fast (sfd-045-221) is played as a Reaction AND an enemy spell or ability targets a friendly unit or gear THEN counter that spell or ability entirely (protects Leona - Zealot and buffed units from targeted removal).
