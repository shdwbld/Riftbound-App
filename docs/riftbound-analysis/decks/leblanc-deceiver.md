# LeBlanc — Deceiver

**Tier:** T1 · **Domains:** Mind / Order · **Win condition:** Exploit Deathknell triggers from dying Reflection tokens — generated every time you conquer or hold a battlefield — to snowball card advantage, rune economy, and damage while Karthus doubles every trigger, then close with Ruined Rex and Rift Herald.

## Mulligan

**Keep:**
- Watchful Sentry (`ogn-096-298`) — cheapest Deathknell body; always worth a copy opening hand.
- Soaring Scout (`ogn-216-298`) — rune channeling on death, enables turns 3–4 play from energy gained.
- Karthus - Eternal (`ogn-236-298`) — the deck's multiplier; if you see it early keep it unless hand is critically clunky.
- Glasc Mixologist (`sfd-165-221`) — sustains Karthus recursion; essential mid-game engine piece.
- LeBlanc - Fragmented (`unl-172-219`) — powerful attacker with Deathknell draw; keep alongside any cheap unit.

**Toss:**
- Sacrifice (`unl-173-219`) — requires a Mighty unit (5+ Might) already in play; dead early. Discard first to generate Reflection tokens via LeBlanc's ability.
- Harnessed Dragon (`ogn-234-298`) / Rift Herald (`unl-179-219`) — too expensive to see turns 1–3; safe discard, may come back via Glasc Mixologist.
- Cull the Weak (`ogn-209-298`) — situational removal; symmetrical effect can harm you early if your Deathknell chain isn't set up.
- Black Rose Dignitary (`unl-152-219`) — slow value; only 1–2 copies in main and is the first mid-cost card to discard for tokens.
- Thousand-Tailed Watcher (`ogn-116-298`) — pure finisher, never a keeper in opening hand.
- Baited Hook (`ogn-242-298`) — powerful but only activates with a unit already on board; secondary keep.

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish presence and prime Deathknell chain

**Concrete line:** Deploy Watchful Sentry (`ogn-096-298`, E:2) or Soaring Scout (`ogn-216-298`, E:2) to any open battlefield. Do not attack into defended positions. If holding 2+ cheap units in hand, split between two battlefields. The goal is to have at least one unit that will die profitably.

**Intended outcome:** Board presence on one or two battlefields; preserve your hand — do not exhaust LeBlanc's ability yet since no valuable unit exists to copy.

---

### Turn 2 — Develop curve; begin scoring

**Concrete line:** Play Karthus - Eternal (`ogn-236-298`, E:3) to your base if you have him. If not, play Black Rose Dignitary (`unl-152-219`, E:3) to an attacking battlefield. Contest a battlefield you can win this turn to conquer and score 1 point. After conquering, exhaust LeBlanc + discard 1 (discard Sacrifice or a redundant copy): play a Reflection token of your best unit on that battlefield, giving it Temporary.

**Intended outcome:** First point scored; first Reflection token on board; Karthus (if played) primes double Deathknell for all future turns.

---

### Turn 3 — Double Deathknell triggers ignite

**Concrete line:** The Reflection token from turn 2 expires at your Beginning Phase, triggering its Deathknell (e.g. Watchful Sentry token draws 1; Karthus makes it draw 2). Attack with your main unit; conquer or hold the same battlefield for another point. Exhaust LeBlanc again (discard another low-value card) to generate a new Reflection of your highest-Might unit. If Karthus is in base, all Deathknells from dying tokens now trigger twice.

**Intended outcome:** 2–3 points on the board; 2–4 cards drawn just from token expiration; rune economy accelerating.

---

### Turn 4 — Mid-game engine: Glasc + LeBlanc - Fragmented

**Concrete line:** Play LeBlanc - Fragmented (`unl-172-219`, E:3, Might:3 / 4 as attacker) to a contested battlefield. Attack; if she wins the showdown and dies, Deathknell draws 1 (or 2 if Beginning Phase, doubled by Karthus to draw 4). Use LeBlanc's legend ability to copy Glasc Mixologist (`sfd-165-221`) or LeBlanc - Fragmented as a Reflection token. Alternatively, drop Baited Hook (`ogn-242-298`, E:3) as gear; use its exhaust ability to trade a Soaring Scout into a search for Ruined Rex.

**Intended outcome:** Card parity or advantage; Glasc Mixologist's Deathknell recycles a fallen unit from trash; Ruined Rex or Rift Herald now in hand for next turn.

---

### Turn 5 — Ruined Rex / Rift Herald pressure spike

**Concrete line:** Play Ruined Rex (`unl-067-219`, E:6, Might:6) to a contested battlefield. Ruined Rex's Deathknell deals 4 to an enemy unit on death (8 with Karthus active — or 4 to two different units). Immediately use LeBlanc's ability post-conquer to create a Ruined Rex Reflection token. Next Beginning Phase: token expires, dealing 4 (or 8 with Karthus) to an enemy unit again. If Glasc Mixologist's Deathknell triggers the same turn, replay Soaring Scout or Watchful Sentry free from trash to maintain board.

**Alternatively:** Play Rift Herald (`unl-179-219`, E:8, Might:7). On entering a battlefield, look at top 3 Main Deck cards, draw a unit. If it dies, Deathknell lets you play a unit from hand to base ignoring Energy cost (pay Power only).

**Intended outcome:** Dominant board presence; enemy units pressured or killed by Rex's Deathknell pings; 4–6 points total.

---

### Turn 6+ — Close with Harnessed Dragon or Sacrifice combo

**Concrete line — Harnessed Dragon close:** Play Harnessed Dragon (`ogn-234-298`, E:8, Might:6): on play, kill an enemy unit — eliminating their blocker. Move to an uncontested battlefield; conquer for 2 points if two battlefields uncontested. Use LeBlanc to copy Dragon as a Reflection token for yet another kill trigger on expiration.

**Concrete line — Sacrifice close:** Once you have a 5+ Might unit (Ruined Rex, Rift Herald, Harnessed Dragon, Thousand-Tailed Watcher) alive, react with Sacrifice (`unl-173-219`, E:1) — killing that Mighty unit to draw 2 and channel 1 rune exhausted. The Deathknell of the sacrificed unit still triggers (doubled by Karthus). Pair with Mirror Image (`unl-200-219`, E:3) to copy a key enemy unit ready to your base; attack enemy battlefields or score 2 points in a single turn before it expires.

**Concrete line — Thousand-Tailed Watcher:** Play TTW (`ogn-116-298`, E:7, Accelerate) — give all enemy units -3 Might this turn (minimum 1). Every enemy unit too weak to block. Attack all battlefields simultaneously with your units to force the final score tally.

**Intended outcome:** Game ends; you hold or conquer the final battlefield needed; opponent cannot react without matching removal at instant speed.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you conquer or hold a battlefield on your turn THEN you may exhaust LeBlanc and discard 1 card to play a ready Reflection token that copies any unit at that battlefield and has Temporary.
- IF a Reflection token's controller's Beginning Phase arrives THEN the token is killed (Temporary), triggering its Deathknell effect.
- IF Karthus - Eternal is in your base THEN every Deathknell effect triggers one additional time (i.e., all Deathknells trigger twice total).
- IF Watchful Sentry (or its Reflection token) dies THEN draw 1 card (draw 2 with Karthus in base).
- IF Soaring Scout (or its Reflection token) dies THEN channel 1 rune exhausted (channel 2 with Karthus in base).
- IF Black Rose Dignitary (or its Reflection token) dies THEN channel 1 rune exhausted (channel 2 with Karthus in base).
- IF LeBlanc - Fragmented dies and it is NOT your Beginning Phase THEN draw 1 card.
- IF LeBlanc - Fragmented dies during your Beginning Phase THEN draw 2 cards (draw 4 with Karthus in base).
- IF LeBlanc - Fragmented dies and Dusk Rose Lab kills her at Beginning Phase before scoring THEN draw 2 (or 4) before points are tallied, giving maximum card advantage.
- IF Ruined Rex (or its Reflection token) dies THEN deal 4 damage to an enemy unit (deal 8, or split 4+4 across two enemy units, with Karthus in base).
- IF Glasc Mixologist (or its Reflection token) dies THEN you may play a unit with Energy ≤3 and no more than 1 Power cost (any color) from your trash, ignoring its Energy cost.
- IF Glasc Mixologist's Deathknell triggers with Karthus in base THEN the effect triggers twice — you may play two qualifying units from trash.
- IF Harnessed Dragon is played THEN you immediately kill one enemy unit (on-play trigger, not Deathknell — happens when you cast it).
- IF a Harnessed Dragon Reflection token expires (Temporary death at Beginning Phase) THEN its Deathknell does NOT kill an enemy unit (Dragon's kill is an "on-play" effect, not Deathknell; Reflection death triggers actual Deathknells only).
- IF Rift Herald moves to a battlefield THEN look at the top 3 cards of your Main Deck and may draw a revealed unit.
- IF Rift Herald (or its Reflection token) dies THEN play a unit from your hand to your base, ignoring its Energy cost (you still pay its Power cost).
- IF Baited Hook (`ogn-242-298`) is exhausted while paying 1 Energy + 1 Order rune THEN kill a friendly unit and search top 5 for a unit with Might up to 1 higher than the killed unit, playing it free.
- IF you use Baited Hook to kill Soaring Scout (Might 1) THEN you may fetch and play a Might-2 unit (e.g., Watchful Sentry) for free from top 5.
- IF Mirror Image is cast targeting an enemy unit THEN play a ready Temporary Reflection copy of that enemy to your base; the copy is ready immediately, enabling it to attack a different battlefield or score 2 points that same turn before expiring.
- IF a Mirror Image copy of an enemy unit expires THEN its Deathknell triggers (using your cards' Deathknell text, not the original owner's).
- IF Sacrifice is played as a Reaction killing a Mighty friendly unit (5+ Might) THEN draw 2 and channel 1 rune exhausted — AND the killed unit's own Deathknell still triggers normally.
- IF Dusk Rose Lab is your active battlefield AND it's the start of your Beginning Phase THEN you may kill a friendly unit there to draw 1 — enabling you to trigger Deathknells earlier than natural token expiration.
- IF Windswept Hillock is the active battlefield THEN all units there gain Ganking (they can move from battlefield to battlefield), allowing Reflection tokens to attack a second battlefield before expiring.
- IF Forbidding Waste is the active battlefield AND a unit is defending alone there THEN it has -2 Might, making lone defenders easier to kill and trigger Deathknells.
- IF Thousand-Tailed Watcher is played THEN all enemy units get -3 Might this turn (minimum 1 Might), neutralizing large blockers and allowing your units to conquer through.
- IF Cull the Weak is played THEN each player kills one of their own units — use this when your Deathknell unit's death value exceeds the opponent's loss (e.g., killing Glasc Mixologist to trigger its Deathknell while opponent loses a vanilla unit).
