# Diana, Scorn of the Moon — Showdown-Dominant Midrange

**Tier:** T1 · **Domains:** Mind / Chaos · **Win condition:** Snowball early showdown leads with Ravenbloom Student + spell-chain tempo, then close with a positioned Hwei whose discard engine provides flexible mid-game power or card advantage, backed by Diana Lunari's Predict filtering to ensure spells on demand.

## Mulligan

**Keep:** Ravenbloom Student (your primary turn-2 threat, scales rapidly with each spell you chain), Stacked Deck (1-Energy filtration sets up your next draw and enables the Frigid Jewel second-draw trigger), Gust (reactive protection that also enables the "play a spell → buff Ravenbloom Student" loop), any single rune of each domain (ensures on-curve energy plays).

**Toss:** Mindsplitter (7-cost, dead in opening hand, tutor to it via Stacked Deck later), Vex - Apathetic (4-cost anti-unit tech only relevant after board is established), Hard Bargain (matchup-specific counter-spell; keep only if opponent is spell-heavy), Fading Memories (situational removal, not an opener), Moonfall (effective only when you already have board presence), Star-Crossed (reactive tempo tool, better in mid-game than turn 1).

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish filtering / light rune play

Play a rune (Mind or Chaos) to bank 1 energy. If you hold Stacked Deck and 1 energy, play it: look at top 3, pull the most impactful card for turn 2 (prioritise Ravenbloom Student or another Stacked Deck), recycle the rest. Keep Gust and Stupefy in hand as reactive protection. Intended outcome: enter turn 2 with 2+ energy and a sculpted hand.

### Turn 2 — Land Ravenbloom Student; fire first spell chain

Deploy Ravenbloom Student (ogn-103-298) at your strongest battlefield. Immediately play Gust or Stupefy as a reaction if the opponent challenges — each spell fires the Student's "when you play a spell, give me +1 Might this turn" trigger, making her a 3S or 4S threat on turn 2. If the showdown fires, Diana Lunari's ability triggers: pay 1 energy, Predict, reveal the top card — if it's a spell, draw it free. Intended outcome: win or trade favorably in the first showdown, bank 1 point lead.

### Turn 3 — Expand board; position Tideturner

Play Tideturner (ogn-199-298) to a battlefield. Use its enter-the-battlefield swap: move Tideturner to the location of an already-deployed Ravenbloom Student or another unit, sending that unit to Tideturner's original position. This repositions your threats without spending an action. Trigger Tideturner's Hidden keyword (cost: any rune, react later for 0) to conceal it for future surprise blocks or surprise conquers. Intended outcome: two battlefields covered, one unit hidden as a trap.

### Turn 4 — Deploy Hwei; begin discard engine

Play Hwei - Brooding Painter (unl-080-219). On move, Hwei draws 1 then discards 1, and the discarded card type determines the bonus: discard a spell to draw 1 more (net +1 card), discard a gear to ready up to 2 runes (acceleration), discard a unit to gain +3 Might this turn (8S total for a conquer threat). Choose the discard based on game state. Play Fizz - Trickster (sfd-140-221) if hand allows: Fizz lets you replay a spell from trash (Energy cost ≤3, free energy) — pairing Fizz with Stupefy or Gust is a free reactive spell from the trash. Intended outcome: card advantage or overwhelming conquer threat, second point.

### Turn 5+ — Close with Mindsplitter / Vex lock / Moonfall

If opponent is clinging to a battlefield, deploy Vex - Apathetic (unl-150-219): her Deflect means opponents must pay 1 Any Rune to target her with spells/abilities, and she Stuns any opponent unit played while she's in the battlefield (they can't move and deal no combat damage). Layer Vex - Cheerless (sfd-146-221) in the same showdown when possible: while she's in combat, friendly spells cost 1 Any Rune less and enemy spells cost 1 Any Rune more, creating a decisive tempo gap. Use Moonfall (unl-198-219) to pull an enemy unit to your contested battlefield and give all enemies there -2 Might. When the opponent's hand is a problem, slam Mindsplitter (ogn-192-298): force a full reveal, choose and discard their best card. Close points with Last Rites on Hwei for a recycled gear threat, or Ride the Wind to move Hwei after a discard trigger activates. Intended outcome: hand disruption + board lock secure the final point(s).

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you play any spell while Ravenbloom Student (ogn-103-298) is at a battlefield THEN she gains +1 Might this turn (stackable — three spells in a chain = +3 Might that turn).
- IF a showdown begins at a battlefield where Diana - Lunari (unl-079-219) is present AND you pay 1 Energy THEN Predict, then reveal the top card of your Main Deck — if it is a spell, draw it for free.
- IF Hwei - Brooding Painter (unl-080-219) moves THEN draw 1, discard 1, then apply the discard bonus: spell → draw 1 additional; gear → ready up to 2 runes; unit → +3 Might this turn.
- IF you discard a spell when Hwei moves AND play Stacked Deck in the same turn THEN you net +1 card (Hwei draws, discard refuels from Stacked Deck filter), effectively drawing two cards for a combined 2 Energy.
- IF Frigid Jewel (unl-074-219) is equipped to a unit AND you draw your second card this turn (via Stupefy, Diana Lunari, Stacked Deck, or Hwei discard) THEN give a friendly unit +2 Might that turn.
- IF Fizz - Trickster (sfd-140-221) enters the field THEN you may play a spell from your trash with Energy cost ≤3 for free (you still pay the Power cost); that spell is Recycled after use.
- IF you use Fizz to replay Gust or Stupefy from trash THEN you trigger Ravenbloom Student's +1 Might and get the Gust/Stupefy effect at zero additional Energy cost.
- IF Vex - Apathetic (unl-150-219) is at a battlefield AND an opponent plays a unit there THEN that unit is Stunned (cannot move, deals no combat damage this turn).
- IF an opponent attempts to target Vex - Apathetic with a spell or ability THEN they must first pay 1 Any Rune (Deflect keyword) — deny them tempo by forcing rune expenditure.
- IF Vex - Cheerless (sfd-146-221) is in combat THEN all friendly spells cost 1 Any Rune less (minimum 1) and all enemy spells cost 1 Any Rune more, creating a 2-rune swing on every spell exchange during that showdown.
- IF you play Gust (ogn-169-298) at instant speed (Reaction) THEN return a unit with 3 Might or less to its owner's hand; use this to cancel an opponent's attack or remove a blocker mid-combat-chain.
- IF you play Stupefy (ogn-095-298) as a Reaction THEN the target unit's Might is reduced by 1 (minimum 1) and you draw 1 card — this both draws into your spell chain and shrinks a threat simultaneously.
- IF Tideturner (ogn-199-298) enters play THEN you may swap it with any other friendly unit at another location, repositioning your board for free without using a separate move action.
- IF you have Hidden Tideturner and an opponent attacks a battlefield THEN you may react by paying 0 Energy to reveal Tideturner and join combat as a surprise defender or attacker.
- IF you play Star-Crossed (unl-128-219) as a Reaction THEN return one friendly unit and one enemy unit to their owners' hands; use mid-chain to bounce a unit with a strong ETB (like Hwei or Fizz) back to hand and replay it, while also removing a key opponent threat.
- IF you replay Hwei via Star-Crossed (bounce → replay) THEN Hwei's move-trigger fires again: draw 1, discard 1, gain discard bonus — getting repeated card advantage or Might spikes from a single Hwei.
- IF Moonfall (unl-198-219) is played THEN choose a battlefield where you have units: you may pull one enemy unit to that battlefield AND give all enemy units there -2 Might this turn; use this to drag the opponent's biggest unit into an unfavorable fight.
- IF Moonfall is used to pull an enemy unit next to a Ravenbloom Student who has been buffed this turn THEN the Student attacks with inflated Might against a Might-reduced enemy, often winning trades cleanly.
- IF Hard Bargain (sfd-136-221) is played as a Reaction THEN counter a spell unless its controller pays 2 Energy; pay an additional 2 Energy yourself to Repeat the counter effect targeting a second spell in the same chain.
- IF Mindsplitter (ogn-192-298) enters play THEN choose an opponent, who reveals their full hand — you choose and discard their single most threatening card.
- IF Fading Memories (ogn-180-298) targets an opponent's key unit or gear THEN it gains Temporary (destroyed at the start of its controller's next Beginning Phase, before scoring) — neutralising a dangerous unit without needing a combat trade.
- IF Last Rites (sfd-150-221) is equipped to Hwei via its Equip cost (Chaos Rune + Recycle 2 from trash) THEN Hwei gains Last Rites' Equip bonus for sustained presence; Hwei's move draw-discard trigger continues to generate value each move.
- IF Diana - Scorn of the Moon is your Legend AND you hold a healthy rune pool (6 Mind + 6 Chaos) THEN her passive ensures your spell-heavy deck can fire multiple Reactions in a single chain without running dry, and the energy generated from her showdown-start ability replenishes spent resources.
