# Vex — Gloomist (Control · Calm/Chaos Attrition)

**Tier:** T2 · **Domains:** Calm + Chaos · **Win condition:** Exhaust Vex on every hold to snowball card advantage, control the board through stuns and bounce, then bury opponents in late-game threats (Evelynn, Kha'Zix, Blast Cone) while denying their scoring windows.

## Mulligan

**Keep:** Teemo - Scout (ogn-197-298), Mutated Mouser (unl-036-219), Existential Dread (unl-134-219), Back Off (unl-042-219) or Gust (ogn-169-298) (one reactive stun/bounce), Evelynn - Entrancing (unl-141-219) to hide face-down.

**Toss:** Kha'Zix - Mutating Horror (unl-143-219) without board presence to trigger its Ambush bonus, Sona - Harmonious (ogn-073-298) without runes on board to ready, Switcheroo (sfd-145-221) in early turns before units are deployed, Emperor's Divide (sfd-043-221) in aggressive matchups where you need reactive answers not evacuation tools, high-energy gear (Edge of Night, Boots of Swiftness) when you lack early units to attach them to.

**Why:** Vex's draw engine only fires on hold triggers — you need cheap early units on battlefields by turn 2. Teemo and Mutated Mouser give instant battlefield presence. Without a reactive disruption piece (Existential Dread / Gust / Back Off) in hand you are punished hard by aggro before the engine starts.

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish battlefield presence + hide Evelynn

Play Teemo - Scout (ogn-197-298): pay :rb_rune_rainbow: to hide it face-down (Bandle Tree gives a bonus hide slot). Alternatively deploy Mutated Mouser (unl-036-219) to a battlefield for a 3/4 defender (2 base + Shield 2). If Evelynn - Entrancing (unl-141-219) is in hand, hide it face-down at any battlefield.

Intended outcome: A unit is on at least one battlefield by end of turn. Teemo faces the threat of a +3 Might burst when revealed. Evelynn is loaded as a reaction. Vex cannot fire yet but the board is primed.

### Turn 2 — Hold, trigger Vex, begin rune charging

Hold with the deployed unit; exhaust Vex - Gloomist (unl-193-219) to draw 1. Play a second unit — Treasure Hunter (sfd-130-221) moves to generate a Gold token exhausted, or Ember Monk (ogn-167-298) if a Hidden card was played. If Sona - Harmonious (ogn-073-298) is on board, her end-of-turn ability readies up to 4 friendly runes — sequence Startipped Peak holds (ogn-288-298) after Sona to chain-channel runes.

Intended outcome: +1 card from Vex hold trigger. Gold token from Treasure Hunter begins rune-acceleration loop. Rune availability climbs toward the reactions needed in turns 3-4.

### Turn 3 — Deploy disruption layer; deny first opponent scoring attempt

If an opponent is about to hold or conquer: reveal Evelynn from face-down (unl-141-219) — move an enemy unit from a different battlefield to Evelynn's battlefield, forcing an unexpected showdown on Vex's terms. Use Existential Dread (unl-134-219) at 1 energy to Stun an attacking enemy unit; if it was already stunned, return it to hand. Use Gust (ogn-169-298) as Reaction to bounce a unit with 3 Might or less.

Play Kha'Zix - Mutating Horror (unl-143-219) via Ambush (Reaction to a battlefield where you have units) — if the enemy unit is alone, Kha'Zix gains +2 Might and 2 XP this turn.

Intended outcome: Opponent scores fewer or no points. Vex fires again on any hold this turn (+1 card). Kha'Zix XP starts accumulating toward level-up.

### Turn 4 — Consolidate board control; activate Blast Cone / Vex - Apathetic

Deploy Vex - Apathetic (unl-150-219): her passive Stuns every new enemy unit that enters her battlefield and locks its movement that turn, effectively shutting down opponent unit deployment at one location. Equip Edge of Night (sfd-139-221) to a unit for Backline protection if aggro is threatening.

Use Back Off (unl-042-219) from Hidden (hide cost :rb_rune_rainbow:, play for 0) to Stun a unit and draw 1. Back Off from hand also draws. Pyke - Returned (unl-145-219) from face-down in a contested showdown: he earns a Gold token each time an enemy unit dies while he is present, accelerating rune income.

Intended outcome: Vex fires multiple times (one exhaust per hold). Hand size is 7-8 cards. Opponent is locked out of one battlefield by Vex - Apathetic. Rune pool is deep enough to float reactions freely.

### Turn 5+ — Bury opponents in attrition; convert card advantage to points

Emperor's Divide (sfd-043-221) from face-down: move all friendly units at a battlefield to base as a free Action — used to dodge an overcommitted enemy showdown, then re-deploy for a free hold on an abandoned battlefield.

Switcheroo (sfd-145-221) from Hidden: swap Might values of two units at the same battlefield, turning Kha'Zix's buffed Might into a lethal attacker or flipping enemy stats. Star-Crossed (unl-128-219) as Reaction: bounce both a friendly and an enemy unit — used to protect a key unit or reset Evelynn/Teemo for another Hidden activation.

Discipline (opp-058-298) as Reaction in showdowns: give a unit +2 Might and draw 1, turning marginal combats into wins.

Intended outcome: Vex has drawn 8-12 extra cards over the game. Opponents are card-starved. Slow conquest of all 3 battlefields by attrition with no viable counter-play remaining.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you or an ally hold any battlefield on your turn THEN exhaust Vex - Gloomist (unl-193-219) to draw 1 card (once per hold trigger, any battlefield).
- IF an opponent plays a unit at a battlefield where Vex - Apathetic (unl-150-219) is present THEN that unit is Stunned and cannot be moved this turn.
- IF Evelynn - Entrancing (unl-141-219) is revealed from face-down on your turn THEN you may move one enemy unit from any other battlefield to Evelynn's battlefield.
- IF Kha'Zix - Mutating Horror (unl-143-219) attacks or defends AND the enemy unit is alone at that battlefield THEN Kha'Zix gains +2 Might this turn and you gain 2 XP.
- IF Kha'Zix - Mutating Horror (unl-143-219) is played from hand as an Ambush (Reaction) THEN it enters as a defender at a battlefield where you already have units.
- IF an enemy unit dies at a battlefield where Pyke - Returned (unl-145-219) is present THEN you play one Gold gear token exhausted (usable as a rune by paying :rb_exhaust: on it).
- IF Back Off (unl-042-219) is played from your hand (not from face-down) THEN you draw 1 in addition to Stunning the target.
- IF Back Off (unl-042-219) is hidden face-down THEN it costs 0 energy to play as a Reaction and still Stuns the target (no draw).
- IF Existential Dread (unl-134-219) targets an enemy unit that is already Stunned THEN instead of Stunning it again, return it to its owner's hand.
- IF you pay the additional :rb_energy_2: Repeat cost on Existential Dread THEN the effect resolves twice, potentially first Stunning and then immediately bouncing the same unit.
- IF Treasure Hunter (sfd-130-221) moves to any location THEN you play a Gold gear token exhausted at the new location.
- IF Sona - Harmonious (ogn-073-298) is at a battlefield at the end of your turn THEN you may ready up to 4 friendly runes.
- IF you hold at Startipped Peak (ogn-288-298) THEN you may channel 1 rune exhausted (in addition to Vex's draw trigger).
- IF you hold at Amateur Recital (unl-207-219) THEN you may move a unit at any battlefield to its base (remove a blocker or reset your own unit for re-entry).
- IF you hold at Star Spring (unl-215-219) AND the first non-token unit is played there this turn THEN you may move another friendly unit at that battlefield to its base.
- IF Bandle Tree (pr-278a-298) is your battlefield THEN you may hide one additional card face-down there (enabling more Hidden activations per turn).
- IF Edge of Night (sfd-139-221) is played from face-down THEN it immediately attaches to a unit you control at that battlefield for free.
- IF Switcheroo (sfd-145-221) is played from Hidden THEN it swaps the Might of two units at the same battlefield for 0 energy as a free Action.
- IF Emperor's Divide (sfd-043-221) is played from Hidden THEN you may move any number of friendly units at a battlefield to their base for 0 energy as a free Action (emergency retreat or battlefield abandonment).
- IF Vex - Mocking (unl-055-219) is on board AND you Stun an enemy unit at any battlefield THEN you may move Vex - Mocking to that battlefield.
- IF Vex - Cheerless (sfd-146-221) is in combat THEN all friendly spells cost :rb_energy_1::rb_rune_rainbow: less (min :rb_energy_1:) and all enemy spells cost :rb_energy_1::rb_rune_rainbow: more.
- IF Nami - Headstrong (unl-052-219) is played WITH the additional :rb_rune_calm: cost THEN Stun an enemy unit on entry; when Nami then holds, the next unit you play this turn is readied and Buffed.
- IF Ember Monk (ogn-167-298) is on board AND you play any card from a Hidden (face-down) state this turn THEN Ember Monk gains +2 Might this turn.
- IF Discipline (opp-058-298) is played as a Reaction in a showdown THEN target unit gains +2 Might this turn and you draw 1.
- IF Star-Crossed (unl-128-219) is played as a Reaction THEN both a friendly unit and an enemy unit are returned to their owners' hands simultaneously.
