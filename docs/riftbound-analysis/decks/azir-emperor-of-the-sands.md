# Azir — Aggro Sand Soldier Swarm

**Tier:** T2 · **Domains:** Calm / Order · **Win condition:** Flood three battlefields with equipped Sand Soldier tokens every turn, conquer points faster than the opponent can stabilise, then close with Azir - Sovereign pulling the whole swarm to a single contested battlefield.

## Mulligan

**Keep (going first):**
- Any 1-cost Equipment (Doran's Shield, Eye of the Herald) — enables Turn 1 Sand Soldier via the legend exhaust.
- Desert's Call — reliable token production without needing the legend.
- Ornn's Forge in the battlefield slot (if used) — removes the energy squeeze entirely.

**Keep (going second):**
- Same 1-cost Equipment; Seat of Power battlefield shifts card-advantage once you start winning battlefields.
- One Deathgrip or Discipline in hand is fine — reactive tools that replace themselves.

**Toss:**
- B.F. Sword on opening hand (too expensive to enable Turn 1 legend ability, dead until Turn 4+).
- Arise! in opener unless you already have early Equipment density (it's a Turn 5-6 refill card, not a Turn 1 play).
- En Garde when going first on an empty board (only good when you have a unit already committed).
- Multiple copies of the same gear on the opening hand (diminishing returns; you need a spread).

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish first Sand Soldier

**Concrete line:** Play Doran's Shield (sfd-033-221) or Eye of the Herald (sfd-153-221) to any battlefield. Pay 1 energy + exhaust Azir - Emperor of the Sands (sfd-197-221) legend ability: play a 2M Sand Soldier token to base. Use Equip cost (Calm or Order rune) to attach the gear to the Soldier.

**Intended outcome:** A 2M (or 3M with Doran's Shield's +1M) ready attacker by end of Turn 1. Opponent must respond or concede early tempo. If Ornn's Forge is the chosen battlefield, the Equipment effectively cost 0 energy, freeing 1 energy for a second action.

---

### Turn 2 — Extend the board to a second battlefield

**Concrete line:** Play a second 1-cost Equipment, trigger legend exhaust again for a second Sand Soldier. Move the Turn 1 Soldier to contest the opponent's battlefield. Alternatively, play Desert's Call (sfd-031-221) for 2 energy to generate a second token without using the legend exhaust (saving exhaust for Turn 3 when you may want to play a 2-cost gear).

**Intended outcome:** Two bodies threatening or occupying two different zones. Opponent cannot block both. First conquest window opens if they have no defenders.

---

### Turn 3 — Attach Brutalizer and push for a conquest point

**Concrete line:** Play Brutalizer (sfd-042-221, 2 cost). Trigger legend exhaust for a third Sand Soldier. Attach Brutalizer to the attacking Soldier (via Weaponmaster keyword, the Equip rune cost is waived). That Soldier is now 2M base + 1M Brutalizer + 2M "attached this turn" bonus = 5M attacker. Attack into opponent's battlefield; opponent must trade or lose the point.

If you hold Guards! (sfd-154-221) unrevealed (Hidden), flash it during the showdown to put a readied 2M Soldier into the fight for 0 energy (only the 1 Order rune).

**Intended outcome:** Score first conquest point. Even if the Soldier dies, the gear is not lost — Deathgrip (sfd-163-221) or Deathknell on Sacred Shears recovers value.

---

### Turn 4 — Spread equipment, Hidden Blade disruption

**Concrete line:** Play Sacred Shears (sfd-172-221, 2 cost) to a freshly played Soldier (again via Weaponmaster, free rune attach). Trigger legend exhaust again. Attack across two battlefields simultaneously. Use Hidden Blade (ogn-213-298) held face-down to eliminate any blocker the opponent drops, paying 0 energy to flip it during their reaction window.

If a Soldier with equipment dies: Sacred Shears Deathknell fires (draw 1). Salvage (ogn-224-298) can immediately kill a spent or useless gear to draw again.

**Intended outcome:** Maintain ≥2 contested battlefields. Prevent opponent from safely dropping a large blocker via Hidden Blade. Card advantage via Deathknell and Discipline/Deathgrip keeps hand full despite aggression.

---

### Turn 5+ — Arise! refill or Azir - Sovereign finisher

**Line A — Arise! (sfd-198-221):** If the board has ≥3 Equipment attached (common by Turn 5), Arise! for 6 energy plays three 2M Sand Soldiers and readies two of them immediately. This is a full board reload after a trade, letting you re-contest every battlefield in one action.

**Line B — Azir - Sovereign (sfd-177-221):** Play Azir - Sovereign for 4 energy (or 5 with Accelerate to enter ready). When he attacks, move any number of your token units to his battlefield — consolidating the entire swarm on one contested zone for a decisive showdown. Trifarian War Camp (ogn-294-298) adds +1M to every attacker on that battlefield, including Sovereign himself.

**Line C — Hall of Legends closer:** When conquering Hall of Legends (sfd-210-221), pay 1 energy to ready Azir - Emperor of the Sands legend immediately. This lets you exhaust the legend again in the same window, producing another Sand Soldier token on the conquest turn itself.

**Intended outcome:** Win the third point required to win the game, or put so many bodies on the board that the opponent cannot recover.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- **IF** you have played any Equipment on your turn **THEN** Azir - Emperor of the Sands legend exhaust is unlocked: pay 1 energy + exhaust to play a 2M Sand Soldier token to base.
- **IF** a Sand Soldier has the Weaponmaster keyword (granted by Azir - Emperor of the Sands to all Sand Soldiers) **THEN** its Equip cost is paid with one fewer rune (effectively free rune cost for gear attachment on Soldiers).
- **IF** Brutalizer is attached to a unit on the same turn you equip it **THEN** that unit gains an additional +2M on top of Brutalizer's base +1M (total +3M for the turn it is attached).
- **IF** a unit equipped with Sacred Shears dies **THEN** its Deathknell triggers: draw 1 card.
- **IF** a unit equipped with Guardian Angel would die **THEN** Guardian Angel is destroyed instead, the equipped unit is healed, exhausted, and recalled to base (survives).
- **IF** you activate Arise! and control ≥3 Equipment **THEN** ≥3 Sand Soldier tokens are played, and up to 2 of them are immediately readied (available to attack this turn).
- **IF** Azir - Sovereign attacks **THEN** you may move any number of your token units to Sovereign's battlefield, collapsing the swarm onto a single decisive contest zone.
- **IF** you conquer Hall of Legends **THEN** you may pay 1 energy to immediately ready Azir - Emperor of the Sands, allowing a second legend exhaust activation on the same turn.
- **IF** you conquer Seat of Power **THEN** draw 1 for each other battlefield you or allies currently control (can draw 2 in a 3-battlefield game).
- **IF** a unit is on Trifarian War Camp **THEN** it has +1M (this applies to both your attackers and defenders stationed there, including the attacking Soldier entering combat).
- **IF** Deathgrip is played as a Reaction and a friendly unit is killed **THEN** another friendly unit gains +M equal to the killed unit's Might this turn, and you draw 1.
- **IF** Guards! is hidden (face-down) and you activate it for 0 energy during a showdown **THEN** a 2M Sand Soldier enters the battlefield; you may pay 1 Order rune to have it enter readied and deal combat damage this turn.
- **IF** Hidden Blade is hidden and you activate it for 0 energy during a showdown **THEN** a unit at that battlefield is killed (opponent's or yours as chosen); the unit's controller draws 2.
- **IF** you play En Garde on a friendly unit that is the only unit you control at its battlefield **THEN** it gains +1M immediately plus an additional +1M (total +2M this turn).
- **IF** an opponent plays a spell costing ≤4 energy and ≤1 rune **THEN** Defy counters it for only 1 energy as a Reaction.
- **IF** Ornn's Forge is the active battlefield **THEN** the first non-token gear played by you each turn costs 1 energy less, enabling 1-cost Equipment for 0 energy and preserving the energy budget for the legend exhaust ability.
- **IF** Facebreaker (hidden) is activated during a showdown **THEN** a friendly unit and an enemy unit at the same battlefield are both stunned, dealing no combat damage this turn (used to protect a key piece or neutralise a threat without trading into it).
