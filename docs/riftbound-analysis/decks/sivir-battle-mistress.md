# Sivir — Aurora Ramp (Midrange)

**Tier:** T2 · **Domains:** Body / Chaos · **Win condition:** Ramp to Dazzling Aurora (OGN) by turn 4-5, then free-play Elder Dragon every end-of-turn to dominate all battlefields and hold them for points.

## Mulligan

**Keep:**
- Any 1-cost setup piece: Scryer's Bloom, Stacked Deck, Sabotage — they sculpt your hand and set up ramp.
- Mobilize or Treasure Trove — the core ramp engine; keep both if you have them together.
- Catalyst of Aeons if you already have a ramp piece.

**Toss:**
- Dazzling Aurora — too expensive to open on; wait until ramp is online by turn 3-4.
- Elder Dragon — same; it is the payoff, not the setup.
- Last Rites without any unit target (no Elder Dragon or Mindsplitter in hand yet).
- Lunar Boon — reactive hand-cycling tool, not needed in the opening sequence.
- More than one Catalyst of Aeons — you need resources, not double ramp spells on an empty board.

**Why:** The deck needs to hit its mana and ramp accelerators in turns 1-3 to land Dazzling Aurora one full turn ahead of schedule. A hand with two payoff pieces and no setup spells will simply miss the window.

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Sculpt and Disrupt

Deploy Scryer's Bloom (unl-136-219) exhausted to set up a free Predict-2 + draw next turn.  
Play Stacked Deck (ogn-183-298) to look at top 3, put the best ramp piece into hand, and recycle the rest.  
Use Sabotage (ogn-156-298) if you go first to rip a key non-unit from the opponent's hand before they can play it.  
*Intended outcome:* You see 5-6 cards worth of selection while the opponent develops, leaving you with the best ramp pieces in hand.

### Turn 2 — Begin Ramp

Activate Scryer's Bloom: Kill it, pay 1 energy + exhaust → Predict 2, draw 1, gain 1 XP. Bank that XP against your legend level.  
Play Mobilize (ogn-134-298): Channel 1 rune exhausted. If you have 4+ energy, follow with Treasure Trove (ogn-186-298) so you have a 2-cost gear that will channel another rune when it dies.  
Hold reactive options: Gust (ogn-169-298) is a Reaction that can bounce a ≤3-Might unit back to hand before it conquers.  
*Intended outcome:* Ahead on rune count vs cost curve; 1-2 runes already channeled, board is clean or neutralised.

### Turn 3 — Ramp Acceleration

Play Catalyst of Aeons (ogn-138-298): Channel 2 runes exhausted. If you can't fill both, draw 1 — but with Mobilize + Treasure Trove already deployed you typically can.  
Pop Treasure Trove via its Chaos + exhaust activation: draw 1 and channel 1 rune exhausted.  
Recycle a rune with any spare Chaos → exhaust Sivir legend (sfd-203-221) to play a Gold gear token (sfd-t03) exhausted. The Gold token's kill + exhaust ability later adds a rainbow rune, stretching resources toward Aurora's 9-cost threshold.  
*Intended outcome:* 5-7 effective energy available next turn — enough to land Dazzling Aurora on curve or one turn early.

### Turn 4 — Land Dazzling Aurora

Play Dazzling Aurora (ogn-160-298) for 9 cost (achievable turn 4 with two Mobilize/Catalyst sequences, or turn 5 on a slower draw).  
Pass turn. At end of turn, Aurora's trigger fires: reveal cards from top of Main Deck until a unit appears, banish that unit, play it ignoring cost, recycle the rest. With Elder Dragon (unl-118-219, 12-cost) as the primary target, you effectively get a free 10/12 that kills up to one enemy unit at every location it can reach on entry.  
Place Sivir — Mercenary (sfd-143-221) at a key battlefield if you have spare energy; she gets +2 Might and Ganking when you've spent ≥2 runes this turn.  
*Intended outcome:* A 10/12 Elder Dragon in play for free, with "any damage kills enemy units" — total board dominance established.

### Turn 5+ — Close the Game

Every end of your turn, Aurora fires again: another free Elder Dragon (or other top-decked unit) enters.  
Move Elder Dragon to undefended battlefields to conquer and hold for points.  
When conquering Sigil of the Storm (ogn-287-298), you are forced to recycle a rune — immediately exhaust Sivir legend to convert that recycle into a Gold gear token, then kill the Gold token in response for a rainbow rune. Net cost: 0.  
Use Pack of Wonders (ogn-181-298) to bounce Dazzling Aurora back to hand if the opponent removes it, then re-play it. Aurora is the engine; protecting it is the priority.  
If the opponent rebuilds their hand with control tools, deploy Mindsplitter (ogn-192-298) for 7: they reveal hand, you pick the best non-unit card and they discard it.  
Equip Last Rites (sfd-150-221) to Elder Dragon: pay Chaos + recycle 2 from trash. When Elder Dragon conquers or holds, you may play another unit from your trash (paying its cost) — recycling banished Elder Dragons back into the game.  
*Intended outcome:* Uncontested board presence across 2-3 battlefields generating steady points. With Aspirant's Climb setting the win threshold at 9 points (not 8), the deck has one extra turn of ramp if needed but also extends opponents' routes to victory, favouring the player with the best late-game inevitability.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you recycle a rune (any source, including Mobilize, Catalyst of Aeons, Sigil of the Storm conquer, or any spell with rune-recycle text), THEN you may exhaust Sivir — Battle Mistress (sfd-203-221) to play a Gold gear token (sfd-t03) exhausted for free.
- IF you kill your Gold gear token by tapping and exhausting it, THEN as a Reaction you Add a rainbow rune — generating 1 extra floating resource that can be spent on any cost.
- IF one or more enemy units die during any phase, THEN Sivir — Battle Mistress readies (becomes un-exhausted), allowing you to recycle a rune again and chain another Gold token in the same turn.
- IF Dazzling Aurora (ogn-160-298) is in play, THEN at the end of each of your turns you reveal cards from the top of your Main Deck until a unit appears, banish it, play it for free, and recycle the rest — giving you a free threat every single turn.
- IF Elder Dragon (unl-118-219) is played (via Aurora or normally), THEN you choose up to one enemy unit at each location and deal 1 to them — and because Elder Dragon's keyword says any amount of your damage is enough to kill enemy units, even 1 damage kills any unit.
- IF Sivir — Mercenary (sfd-143-221) is played with the Accelerate cost (1 energy + Chaos rune) on a turn where you have already spent ≥2 runes, THEN she enters ready AND immediately has +2 Might (6 total) and Ganking, letting her move and contest multiple battlefields in one turn.
- IF Treasure Trove (ogn-186-298) leaves the board (killed manually via Chaos + exhaust, or by any removal), THEN its owner draws 1 card and channels 1 rune exhausted — providing ramp-on-demand when you need to hit Aurora's 9-cost threshold.
- IF you activate Scryer's Bloom (unl-136-219) by killing it (1 energy + exhaust), THEN you Predict 2 (look at top 2, recycle any, reorder rest) and draw 1, and gain 1 XP — filtering the deck toward Aurora or Elder Dragon while advancing legend level.
- IF Catalyst of Aeons (ogn-138-298) is played and you successfully channel 2 runes, THEN both runes enter exhausted (available next turn as resources) — accelerating Aurora by 2 full turns of income.
- IF you conquer Sigil of the Storm (ogn-287-298), THEN you must recycle one of your runes; use Sivir's passive immediately to convert that forced recycle into a Gold token, turning a tempo penalty into a net-zero or net-positive play.
- IF Pack of Wonders (ogn-181-298) is tapped (exhaust), THEN you return another friendly gear, unit, or facedown card to its owner's hand — use this to protect Dazzling Aurora from removal or to replay Treasure Trove / Scryer's Bloom for additional ramp triggers.
- IF Last Rites (sfd-150-221) is equipped to Elder Dragon (cost: Chaos rune + recycle 2 from trash) AND Elder Dragon conquers or holds a battlefield, THEN you may play a unit from your trash, paying its cost — enabling you to recur banished Elder Dragons or Mindsplitter from earlier turns.
- IF Mindsplitter (ogn-192-298) is played, THEN the chosen opponent reveals their hand and you select one card for them to discard — strip the opponent's removal or counter-ramp pieces before they can answer your Aurora.
- IF Forgotten Monument (sfd-209-221) is in play, THEN players cannot score points at that battlefield until their third turn — delaying the opponent's early-game point acceleration and buying ramp turns.
- IF Aspirant's Climb (ogn-276-298) is in play, THEN the points threshold to win increases by 1 (typically 9 total instead of 8) — extending the game by one scoring cycle, which favours the Aurora ramp deck's end-of-turn free unit engine over aggro decks.
- IF Disposal Order (unl-103-219) is played (Reaction), THEN you either choose up to 3 cards from opponents' trashes and have their owners recycle them (denying trash recursion) OR draw 1 card — use the recycle mode against Last Rites / graveyard decks, use draw mode when you need card velocity.
- IF Lunar Boon (unl-125-219) is played (Reaction: discard 1, draw 2), THEN net draw +1 at any priority window — use on opponent's turn to ambush threats or in a showdown to reload your reactive suite.
- IF Akshan — Mischievous (sfd-109-221, sideboard) is played with the Body + Body rune additional cost, THEN he moves an enemy gear to your base and you control it until he leaves the board; if it is an Equipment it attaches to Akshan — use against Aurora mirror matches or gear-heavy decks.
- IF Fading Memories (ogn-180-298, sideboard) is played targeting any unit or gear at a battlefield, THEN that permanent becomes Temporary and is killed at the start of its controller's next Beginning Phase — answer for sticky threats the normal removal suite cannot touch.
