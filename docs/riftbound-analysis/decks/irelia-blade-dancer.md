## Mulligan

**Keep:**
- Irelia - Fervent (in slower / midrange matchups) — she is the primary conquer threat and gets harder to remove every time she's readied.
- Stellacorn Herder — the card-draw engine; the earlier she hits, the more cards you generate.
- Tideturner — provides a free blocker and repositioning; keeps your board flexible from turn 1.
- One reactive counter-spell (Defy or Not So Fast) — opens up safe threat deployment.
- Ride The Wind — readies a unit for another attack and synergizes with Herder draw.

**Toss:**
- Draven - Audacious alone in an aggressive matchup (6-cost is too slow without early setup).
- Gear cards in the opening hand when you lack the unit to equip them.
- Hard Bargain / Switcheroo without other board presence — these are value tools, not openers.
- Duplicate counter-spells — holding three counters while the opponent builds freely is a tempo loss.

## Turn-by-turn game plan

### Turn 1 — Establish early foothold

Concrete line: Deploy Tideturner face-down (Hidden for 1 rune). Alternatively, if you have Lonely Poro, play it to a safe battlefield. Hold remaining energy for reactive spells. Irelia's legend ability is not yet online (she costs 5 to place in most formats); focus on board presence.

Intended outcome: You have a unit in position or a Hidden card ready to surprise. No meaningful investment into the opponent's board yet; your resources are primarily held for counter-play.

### Turn 2 — Develop threats, hold reactive mana

Concrete line: Play Tideturner (ogn-199-298) from hand, optionally using its enter ability to swap it with a unit already at a battlefield, repositioning for free. Play a second unit if available (Lonely Poro to a contested battlefield). Hold 1-2 energy for En Garde (ogn-046-298) or Discipline (ogn-058-298) in combat.

Intended outcome: Two units on the board creates conquer pressure. Reactive spells force the opponent to commit first. You draw 1 from Discipline if it fires — maintaining hand parity while holding board.

### Turn 3 — Land Stellacorn Herder or key protection spell

Concrete line: Play Stellacorn Herder (opp-048-221) to a battlefield where you have another unit. Move a friendly unit via Ride The Wind (ogn-173-298) or The Syren (ogn-184-298) — each move draws 1. If opponent plays a removal spell targeting your unit, respond with Not So Fast (sfd-045-221) to counter it for free (no energy, just 1 rune).

Intended outcome: Stellacorn Herder begins generating card advantage. Your card pool grows faster than the opponent's. If Herder is removed, you have spent their removal before deploying Irelia - Fervent or Draven - Audacious.

### Turn 4 — Deploy Irelia - Fervent with counter-spell backup

Concrete line: Play Irelia - Fervent (sfd-057-221) to a battlefield you intend to conquer. Activate Irelia (Blade Dancer) legend ability: exhaust Irelia - Blade Dancer and pay 1 rune → ready Irelia - Fervent after she exhausts in the conquer attempt. If opponent casts a spell targeting her, pay the Deflect tax (1 rune) on their behalf making it expensive, or respond with Not So Fast (sfd-045-221) to counter it outright. Equip Guardian Angel (sfd-051-221) if you have a spare Calm rune — if she would die, Guardian Angel dies instead and she is healed and recalled.

Intended outcome: Irelia - Fervent conquers a battlefield and gets +1 Might from her ready trigger, reaching 5 Might. She may attack a second time if the legend ability is used again. Opponent is forced into inefficient trades or must spend two cards to remove her.

### Turn 5+ — Chain conquers, maximize card advantage, close out

Concrete line: Use Irelia (Blade Dancer) legend ability each time a friendly unit attacks — exhaust legend + pay 1 rune → ready the attacker again. After conquering with Irelia - Fervent on Sunken Temple (sfd-218-221), pay 1 energy to draw 1 (Sunken Temple triggers when you conquer with a Mighty unit, 5+ Might). Move Stellacorn Herder with The Syren (ogn-184-298) or Ride The Wind to draw more cards. If Zhonya's Hourglass (ogn-077-298) was placed face-down earlier, activate it for free to save a key unit from death this turn. Deploy Draven - Audacious (sfd-148-221) as a second protected threat — every combat he wins scores 1 additional point. Use Charm (ogn-043-298) to move an enemy blocker off a battlefield before your final conquer attempt.

Intended outcome: Multiple battlefields conquered. Draven - Audacious is generating bonus points every time he wins combat. Opponent is behind on cards, resources, and board presence. The game ends through point accumulation — hold conquered battlefields until victory points are secured.

## Key conditional interactions (IF/THEN)

- IF you use Irelia - Blade Dancer's legend ability (exhaust + pay 1 rune) while a friendly unit is chosen THEN that unit becomes ready immediately, enabling it to attack or block again in the same turn.
- IF you conquer a battlefield while Irelia - Blade Dancer is exhausted THEN you may pay 1 energy to ready Irelia - Blade Dancer herself, letting you use her ability again in the same turn.
- IF Stellacorn Herder (opp-048-221) moves to any location THEN draw 1 card — this triggers on any movement effect including Ride The Wind, Charm, The Syren, Fight or Flight, and Tideturner's enter ability.
- IF Irelia - Fervent (sfd-057-221) is chosen by your own spell or ability (e.g., Discipline, En Garde) THEN she gains +1 Might this turn — stack with combat tricks to reach 6-7 Might.
- IF Irelia - Fervent is readied by the legend ability after conquering THEN she gains another +1 Might trigger, potentially stacking to 6+ Might and conquering a second battlefield in the same turn.
- IF an opponent casts a spell that targets a friendly unit or gear THEN Not So Fast (sfd-045-221) counters it as a reaction at no energy cost (1 rune only).
- IF an opponent targets Irelia - Fervent or Draven - Audacious with a spell or ability THEN they must pay an additional 1 rune (Deflect) before the targeting resolves — making efficient removal cost 2 runes total when combined with Not So Fast.
- IF Zhonya's Hourglass (ogn-077-298) is attached to a unit and that unit would die THEN the Hourglass dies instead; the unit is healed, exhausted, and recalled to its base — effectively a free save when the Hourglass was hidden earlier for 1 rune.
- IF Guardian Angel (sfd-051-221) is equipped to a unit and that unit would die THEN Guardian Angel dies instead; the unit is healed, exhausted, and recalled — another layer of protection that can stack with Hourglass on different units.
- IF Draven - Audacious (sfd-148-221) wins a combat THEN you score 1 additional point — stack with conquers for multiple points per turn cycle.
- IF Draven - Audacious dies in combat THEN the opponent who caused the death scores 1 point — never trade him into a larger blocker without protection.
- IF you conquer Sunken Temple (sfd-218-221) with a Mighty unit (5+ Might) THEN you may pay 1 energy to draw 1 card — use Irelia - Fervent at 5+ Might to trigger this reliably.
- IF a player chooses a unit at The Dreaming Tree (ogn-292-298) with a spell for the first time that turn THEN that player draws 1 card — play your own Discipline or En Garde on your unit here to draw a bonus card.
- IF Rockfall Path (sfd-216-221) is one of your active battlefields THEN no unit can be played directly there — forces opponents to commit to the other two battlefields and lets you control the Rockfall contest solely through movement.
- IF Tideturner (ogn-199-298) enters play THEN you may choose a unit you control at another location, swap Tideturner to that location and the chosen unit back — repositioning both units for free and can trigger Stellacorn Herder if Herder is the swapped unit.
- IF Defy (ogn-045-298) is in hand and opponent plays a spell costing 4 energy or less with 1 rune or less THEN counter it as a reaction — use to protect Irelia - Fervent from removal windows or disrupt opponent's combo turns.
- IF Vex - Cheerless (sfd-146-221) is in combat THEN friendly spells cost 1 energy + 1 rune less (minimum 1 energy) and enemy spells cost 1 energy + 1 rune more — deploy in critical combat to tax opponent's combat tricks while making yours cheaper.
- IF Switcheroo (sfd-145-221, Hidden) is placed face-down for 1 rune THEN it can be activated for free as a reaction to swap the Might values of two units at the same battlefield — use to make a small unit survive combat against a large blocker, or shrink an enemy attacker.
- IF Fight or Flight (ogn-168-298, Hidden) was placed face-down for 1 rune THEN activate for free to move any unit from a battlefield to its base — use to pull out a dying friendly unit before it dies, or remove an enemy blocker before your attack.
- IF Charm (ogn-043-298) targets an enemy unit THEN move that enemy unit to any location — use before a conquer attempt to clear the defending battlefield of blockers.
- IF Hard Bargain (sfd-136-221) is used with its Repeat cost THEN counter two spells for the price of one card — effective against control decks that chain multiple spells on the same stack.
